Welcome to STM(Space - Tech - Magic) modpack! This modpack offers 3 different themes in one semi light weight pack consisting of only less than 100 mods! Each part has a bit for you to sit and enjoy while COVID-19 has us locked inside our houses! So why don't we go over some of the mods this pack has to offer to get an idea. First lets begin with tech, the tech tree its quite large and can be connected together, mods that are included are Thermal Expansion, Mekanism, Nuclearcraft and Ender IO. For magic however isn't the same story us the 3 mods (and one addon) we have are completely unrelated to each other those include Blood Magic , Astral Sorcery and Bewitchment. Space huh who though it could be a space themed modpack, for space we only have galacticraft however its much bigger due to the 2 addons we have installed you can explore our entire solar system and even further! Have fun!
